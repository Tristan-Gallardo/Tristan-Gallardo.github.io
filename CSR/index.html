CSR Matters

CSR, wich mean Corporate Social Responsibility, matters because it ensures that companies operate ethically, contribute to sustainable development, and consider the interests of all stakeholders — not just shareholders.
By doing so, it can enhance a company's reputation, attract customers, and help recruit and retain top talent.
