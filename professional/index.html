Professional

Here you will find my strengths as well as my way to adapt to challenges.

Strengths

To describe my strength simply, I'm a perfectionist.

I'm especially good at math and everything related to coding; it has been my passion since I was a kid, and I take them both as a game.

I'm also good at imagining and creating things; this mostly helps me when creating user interfaces, understanding what the user will like the most.

Lastly, I'm good at cooperating too, working easily in a team, and facilitating the work of others.

My weakness is mostly talking vocally about myself, as I am shy, but thanks to my internships and work-study programs, I'm becoming better and better at presenting myself and my projects.

Adaptability

While I take a little bit of time to adapt to challenges, when I take hold, like with projects, I will never give up.

Indeed, when one of them really gives me difficulties, most of the time I will do research, find if anyone has had the same problems or if there is a way around it, and try to apply a modified version of their answer.

When AI chats appeared, I also found some utilities in them too.

For example, instead of going through all documentations, I could simply ask them if a certain function existed, saving time, or, if they are advanced enough, ask them how to repair some functions.

And even then, if any of these potential sources of aid didn't help me, I will still be determined and I will try until I find the solution, as I don't like to give an unfinished/imperfect project.
